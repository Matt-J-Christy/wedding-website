Getting to Boston



We suggest flying in Boston Logan International Airport as it is 15 minutes from the city. When you arrive in Boston, a taxi is usually the quickest option to getting into the city and the price is comparabel to uber. You can also take the MBTA (Blue Line), which will be the cheapest option but it will take around 40 minutes to get to the city.
