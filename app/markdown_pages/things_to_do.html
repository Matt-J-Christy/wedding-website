Boston Neighborhoods


North End is the old Italian neighborhood. You usually can't go wrong with any Italian restaurant, cafe, or bakery there.

South End is a trendy neighborhood with a lot of great restaurants.

Back Bay has a lot of shopping and cute bars to pop in and out of during the day.

Seaport is another good neighborhood to shop and have a drink on the waterfront.
